About Us

We are a multidisciplinary TEAM

Our team is composed of researchers specializing in different technology fields. Researchers from Aalborg University (Denmark), Lappeenranta University of Technology (Finland), and University of Waterloo (Canada) joined efforts to propose TRUEDIP in order to support the most vulnerable population with unique features that only Blockchain can provide.
